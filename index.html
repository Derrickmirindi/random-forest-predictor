About this tool

Predicts the split tensile strength (MPa) of rubberized brick-powder concrete from seven mix-design and curing parameters using a trained Random Forest model (200 trees), with a 90% prediction band and an ensemble-spread estimate. Runs entirely in your browser — no uploads.

Model details

Algorithm: Random Forest Regressor - ensemble of 200 decision trees (max depth 10), averaging per-tree predictions; intrinsically nonlinear and robust to feature interactions.

Inputs (7): Cement, Brick powder, Water, Fine aggregate, Coarse aggregate, Waste tire rubber, Age.

Output: Split tensile strength (MPa), the mean over all decision trees (no scaling required).
